Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 January 2026

AuraFlow respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the AuraFlow mobile application on any supported platform, including iOS and Android.

Information We Do Not Collect

AuraFlow does not collect, store, or process any personal information.

  • No user accounts
  • No names, email addresses, or identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising data

In-App Purchases

AuraFlow uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases across supported platforms. RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase-related information in order to validate transactions and manage entitlements.

AuraFlow does not have access to personal or payment information. All payments are handled securely by the relevant app store provider, such as Apple App Store or Google Play.

For more information, please review RevenueCat’s privacy policy:
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

Aside from RevenueCat for purchase management, AuraFlow does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

AuraFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
